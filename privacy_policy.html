Privacy Policy

MADECC Construction values your privacy and is committed to safeguarding your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect information obtained through our website and services.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect personal details such as your name, email address, phone number, project information, IP address, and browser data when you interact with our website or contact us directly.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To respond to inquiries and provide construction services
  • To manage projects and client communication
  • To improve website performance and user experience
  • To comply with legal and regulatory requirements

3. Cookies & Analytics

Our website may use cookies and analytics tools (such as Google Analytics) to understand visitor behavior, improve functionality, and support advertising services including Google AdSense.

4. Advertising & Google AdSense

Third-party vendors, including Google, use cookies to serve ads based on users’ prior visits. Google’s use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads relevant to your interests. You may opt out via Google Ad Settings.

5. Data Protection & Security

We implement technical and organizational security measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

6. Your Privacy Rights

You have the right to request access, correction, or deletion of your personal data. To exercise these rights, contact us at madecccons@gmail.com.

7. Policy Updates

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
